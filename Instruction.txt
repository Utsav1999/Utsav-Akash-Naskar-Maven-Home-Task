It is a switch-case program in JAVA language. 
***At first it will give four options:
		Option 1: To see all chocolate items
		Option 2: To see all sweet items
		Option 3: T0 see both chocolate and sweet items
		Option 4: To exit

***After choosing an option it will ask for your price range(except the Option:4)

***After giving price range it will show available items within your price range in ascending order.
